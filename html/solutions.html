Our Solutions

End-to-End Agricultural Systems for African Smallholder Farmers

FarmWiser, Your All-in-One Digital Farming Companion

FarmWiser is PlowAfrika’s integrated platform that connects farmers to advisory, finance, and markets, all in one place.

AI Advisory

  • Crop-specific guidance
  • Climate-smart farming recommendations
  • Pest and disease alerts
  • Localized best practices
  • Advisory delivered digitally and through field agents

Input Credit & Financial Inclusion

  • Access to seeds, fertilizers, and agro-inputs
  • AI-backed credit assessment framework
  • Credit behavior tracking and farmer scoring
  • Transparent repayment structures
  • Designed for first-time and excluded farmers

Market Access

  • Buyer linkages and aggregation support
  • Fair pricing and offtake opportunities
  • Reduced post-harvest losses
  • Better income predictability for farmers

On-Ground Agent Network

  • Field agents support farmers throughout the season
  • Services available for farmers without smartphones
  • Hands-on training, monitoring, and handholding
  • Strengthens trust and adoption
